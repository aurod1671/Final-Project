    • About Embroidery

    Embroidery can definitely be therapeutic. It keeps your fine motor skills sharp and your mind focused. Hand embroidery helps many of us to focus⁠—keeping worries and the chaotic thoughts of our daily life away. It is meditative and has been used as a therapeutic method to improve mental and emotional health.

    The job requires the hand embroiderer to have the skills to stitch a variety of Flat stitches, Loop stitches and Knotted stitches. coordination, motor skills and vision (including near vision, distance vision, colour vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and ability to change focus)
