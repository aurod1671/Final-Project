    • Embroidery

    Embroidery is a type of craft that uses needles to apply thread or yarn to decorate a fabric. The art of embroidery has been dated between the 5th and 3rd centuries bce. There are different types of techniques or stitches. These techniques include: the chain stitch, buttonhole or blanket stitch, running stitch, satin stitch, and cross stitch. To make these stitches people use yarn or embroidery thread. Other materials that are needed are: embroidery hoops, needles, and fabric.
